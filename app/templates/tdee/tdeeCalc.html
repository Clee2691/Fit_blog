About

The TDEE calculator calculates your Total Daily Energy Expenditure. Your TDEE is a measurement of how many calories you burn per day.

By utilizing this information, you can determine how many calories you need to eat to lose, gain, or maintain weight.

This calculator uses the Mifflin St. Jeor equation. It is the most accurate BMR equation to date.

Instructions

  1. Enter your height in feet and inches.
  2. Enter your weight in pounds.
  3. Enter your age.
  4. Select your gender.
  5. Select your activity level. To see what each level corresponds to, hover over the "i" icon. For starters, you can pick sedentary for the most conservative calculation.
  6. Press the calculate button!
